Refund Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Overview

At RBConsult, we strive for customer satisfaction. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be provided for our services.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered based on the project phase:

  • Before project start: 100% refund minus any processing fees
  • During discovery phase: 75% refund of remaining balance
  • During design phase: 50% refund of remaining balance
  • During development phase: No refund, payment for completed work required
  • After delivery: No refund applicable

3. Cancellation Process

To cancel a project:

  • Submit written cancellation request via email
  • Include project details and reason for cancellation
  • Allow 5 business days for processing
  • Receive confirmation and refund details

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:

  • Completed and delivered work
  • Third-party licenses or services purchased on client's behalf
  • Rush delivery fees
  • Consultation fees after service delivery

5. Dispute Resolution

If you're unsatisfied with our services, we encourage you to contact us first to resolve the issue. We're committed to finding a fair solution.

6. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 10-15 business days. The time for funds to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution.
