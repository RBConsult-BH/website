Delivery Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Project Timelines

Project delivery times vary based on scope and complexity:

  • Small projects: 2-4 weeks
  • Medium projects: 4-8 weeks
  • Large projects: 8-16 weeks
  • Enterprise projects: Custom timeline

Exact timelines are provided in the project proposal after requirements analysis.

2. Milestone Delivery

Projects are typically divided into milestones:

  • Discovery & Planning
  • Design & Prototyping
  • Development Phase 1
  • Development Phase 2
  • Testing & Quality Assurance
  • Deployment & Launch

Each milestone has specific deliverables and deadlines outlined in the project agreement.

3. Delivery Methods

We deliver digital products through:

  • Source code repository (GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket)
  • Direct deployment to client servers
  • App store submissions (iOS App Store, Google Play)
  • Cloud hosting platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • Secure file transfer for sensitive projects

4. Acceptance Criteria

Deliverables are considered accepted when:

  • All features match the agreed specifications
  • Testing is complete with issues resolved
  • Documentation is provided as agreed
  • Client provides written acceptance or 7 days pass without feedback

5. Delays and Extensions

In case of delays:

  • We communicate proactively about any potential delays
  • Client-caused delays may extend the timeline
  • Force majeure events are handled case-by-case
  • Rush delivery available for additional fees

6. Post-Delivery Support

After delivery, we provide:

  • 30-day bug fix guarantee
  • Documentation and knowledge transfer
  • Optional maintenance contracts
  • Training sessions if agreed

7. Communication

We maintain regular communication through:

  • Weekly progress updates
  • Milestone completion notifications
  • 24/7 email support
  • Scheduled video calls as needed
