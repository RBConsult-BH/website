What is Adjust?

Adjust is a mobile measurement and analytics platform used by product and growth teams to attribute installs and understand in-app behavior across channels.

It provides attribution, analytics, and fraud prevention so you can see which sources drive value and keep your data clean.

  • Attribution from click to install to key in-app events
  • Cross-channel performance measurement
  • Fraud prevention to protect budgets and data integrity
